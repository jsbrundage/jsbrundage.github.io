About the Author

J.S. Brundage crafts dark fantasy worlds where ancient magic collides with mortal ambition. The Duskwalker Chronicles explores themes of power, sacrifice, and the cost of confronting the shadows that lurk at the edge of civilization.

Drawing inspiration from classic fantasy while pushing into darker, more visceral territory, each book in the series peels back another layer of a world teetering on the brink of collapse.
